Bolder Games Prototypes

A collection of the company's prototypes

Bolder Games has recently pushed efforts to expand their game portfolio. My role in this as lead game developer has been to design and implement a whole bunch for each prototype. I cover systems design and engineering, gameplay programming as well as design, standardizing codebases, usage of experimental technology (unsupported engine-related features needed for the gameplay needs of the game) and plugins(if used at all), and a whole lot more. Below are some of the titles Bolder Games has been working on. NOTE: everything here is under production so unfortunately I can't reveal too many details. Always more coming soon!

Rapture

Rapture is a top down game about surviving a dystopian apocalypse by avoiding massive swarms of invincible enemy spirits. The swarms use a combination of fluid movements with simplified chaos dynamics, particle based collision systems, and make use of HLSL to support massive crowds of up to 80000 units at 40 fps on an NVidia 2070 card. Current art is placeholder but gameplay systems are solid!

SHMUPRPG - Title change pending

SHMUPRPG, as it is currently refered to, is exactly that. It is a 3d Shoot em up game with top down elements, a Space Station the player can reach at the end of every level to customize their ships and get back on the road! Art side is getting some serious changes so I will add images when ready!
