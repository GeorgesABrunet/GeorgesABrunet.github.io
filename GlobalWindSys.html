Global Wind System

Enabling Wind to affect foliage and more in Unreal Engine 4

This tutorial builds upon the Foliage Deformation System as it requires you to have a foliage system set up. On the portfolio page of the website, you'll find that it follows a chain of tutorials, starting at the Persisten Trails one. If you know what you're doing, however, feel free to follow along here and adjust as needed. This tutorial covers how to get global wind to communicate with all the assets you want affected by wind, namely your foliage. This could be an essential addition to your production if you wish to have a world that feels alive through climate.
