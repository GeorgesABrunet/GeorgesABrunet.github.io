Project Myrmidon

Grappling and Gliding

With all the craze of emerging grappling mechanics in games, I decided to create a little project that showcases my take on it. Project Myrmidon sets the player in a world where reality has shattered. Upon the discovery an interesting, powerful crystal found deep beneath the crust of the Earth, rampant industrialization became the only manner to address its existence. Seemingly capable of sustaining inexhaustable energy and a mysterious ability to capture the soul of a being and using that same crystal shard to power an automaton, inanimate things could move and live as souls in automatons.

The Concept
