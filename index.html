Welcome!

My name is Georges Brunet and I’m an aspiring Game Developer based in Colorado. Video games have always played a crucial role in my life and the work behind them fascinates me. Whether the game is a AAA title or something as simple as Pong, I have always seen the charm of the art. I have a background in math and coding that has been a backbone in designing and making the features I would like to see, specifically in the domains of game design and technical art. Most if not all of my creations will be available in the projects and portfolio tabs!

I graduated from the University of Colorado, Boulder’s School of Arts & Sciences with a B.A. in Mathematics with a concentration in Applicability and Statistics in August 2019. Throughout my years at the university I worked as a Data Analytics Intern at Aspen Growth Ventures, a French Tutor at the French and Italian Department of the University of Colorado, Boulder, and as a Data Science Intern at Standard Alcohol, Inc. As of May 2020, I have fully dedicated my time to a career in the games industry.

In my free time, I enjoy trying out new features to implement, watching comedy, and the company of friends and family. I like to make art, whether it be drawings, sculptures or paintings. I watch sports, and my personal favorite: play video games. Have a look around my website and see if my work interests you. If it does, I can be contacted on LinkedIn, or email.
